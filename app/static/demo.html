🤖 How do we pick great homes for you? (tap to open)
  1. We read your wish. A language model reads what you wrote and splits it into must-haves (like "in Zurich", "under 3000 CHF", "has a balcony") and nice-to-haves (like "quiet", "near a park", "family-friendly").
  2. We remove anything that doesn't fit. Homes that break a must-have are dropped right away — they don't compete for the top spots.
  3. We check the words. We look for the keywords from your wish inside each home's title and description.
  4. We look at the pictures. For each home, we ask: "Do these photos match the kind of home you described?" (a computer-vision model compares your wish to every photo.)
  5. We understand the meaning. We also check whether the description of the home means the same thing as your wish — even when the words are different.
  6. We honour your nice-to-haves. One sorted list per wish — quietest first, or cheapest, or closest to transit, and so on.
  7. We mix the lists together. All those rankings vote for each home; the homes that everyone liked bubble up to the top.
  8. We remember you (only if you let us). If you're logged in and Learn from me is on, we use the homes you loved / saved / hid to nudge the list toward what fits your taste.
